Who Is The Five Nation?

The Five Nation was founded by a man named Allah (honorably Clarence Edward Smith) in the early 1960s in Harlem, New York. Allah, a former member of the Nation of Islam. He left to teach the youth the "Knowledge of Self," focusing on self-empowerment, community engagement, and the understanding that every Black man can become his own God. Sharing a deeper understanding of divinity beyond traditional religious structures. In late 1963, Clarence, now known as "Allah", along with his companions Abu Shahid (formerly John 37X), Free Cipher Akbar aka Ol Man Justice C (formerly James Howell), and Hebeka (formerly Eugene 32X), began walking the streets of Harlem, teaching the youth about their divine nature and their potential. By October 1964, Allah officially established the Five Percent Nation. The Five Nation derives its name from the concept that only Five of people possess true knowledge and understanding of themselves and the world, while the remaining 95% are either ignorant or oppressors.
